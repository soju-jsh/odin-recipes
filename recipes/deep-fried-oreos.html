Deep-Fried Oreos

Deep-fried Oreos are a carnival favorite where I grew up. Oreos get dipped into pancake batter and fried â€” yummy! You can do this with or without a deep fryer.

Ingredients

  • Vegetable oil
  • Milk
  • Egg
  • Pancake Mix
  • Package of Oreos

Steps

  1. Make the batter
  2. Dip the cookies in the batter
  3. Fry the cookies in batches until they're golden brown
  4. Dust the finished oreos with powdered sugars
